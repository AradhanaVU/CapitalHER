About Us

We're closing the confidence gap for girls and money.

CapitalHER is a Canadian non-profit equipping girls aged 13–18 with the financial literacy, entrepreneurial skills, and mentorship to build the futures they imagine.

Mission

Equip every girl with financial power.

We exist to give every girl in Canada the financial literacy and entrepreneurial confidence to build the life she chooses — regardless of zip code, income, or what her parents know about money.

Vision

A generation that walks in knowing what she's worth.

We see a Canada where young women enter every room — boardroom, bank, or boardwalk — already fluent in money, comfortable with risk, and unapologetic about ambition.

Values

Bold curiosity. Real-world skills. Sisterhood that scales.

Joyful learning. Radical access. Mentors who tell the truth. We don't gatekeep what we know — and we hold the door open behind us, always.

Why this, why now.

Only 31% of Canadian women feel confident managing investments. Girls are still being raised to be careful with money — when they should be raised to be powerful with it.

We're rewriting that script. One cohort at a time.

Our approach

The how behind the program.

Mission and vision matter. So does method. Here's how we actually deliver it — and why our alumni keep coming back as mentors.

Curriculum built for teen girls

Every module is co-designed with educators, financial professionals, and alumni. Plain language. Real examples. Zero condescension.

Mentorship at the centre

Each participant is matched 1:1 with a woman in business. Mentors are trained, supported, and held to a clear code of care.

Learning through doing

Girls leave with a working business, a financial plan, and a pitch they've delivered to real investors — not just a certificate.
