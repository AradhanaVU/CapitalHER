The participant journey

  1. 01

    Apply

    A short application — no grades or essays. We're looking for curiosity, not credentials.

  2. 02

    Onboard

    Meet your cohort, get matched with a mentor, set personal goals for the 12 weeks.

  3. 03

    Build

    Weekly workshops on money + business. Build your venture from idea to MVP.

  4. 04

    Launch

    Pitch at Demo Day to a panel of women investors. Compete for a share of the $10k funding pool.

  5. 05

    Belong

    Join the alumni network for life — mentorship, jobs, alumni-only events, lifelong sisterhood.
