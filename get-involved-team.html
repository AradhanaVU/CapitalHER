Apply · Volunteer

Two ways to show up for the next generation.

Pick the role that fits your time and skills. We onboard, train, and support every volunteer.

Role 1

Cohort Mentor

~2 hrs/week · 12 weeks

Mentor a small group of girls through their accelerator journey — from idea to Demo Day. Best for women with experience in business, finance, design, or product.

Role 2

Workshop Facilitator

~3 hrs/month · flexible

Lead a single workshop in your area of expertise — taxes, branding, investing, public speaking. Light prep, big impact.

Role 3

Business Plan Reviewer

~1 hr per plan · async

Spend 60 minutes giving written feedback on a real business plan from a CapitalHER participant. We match you with a plan in your industry; you return notes within 7 days on a simple template we provide. Perfect for busy professionals — async, low commitment, high impact.
