Ready to join a cohort?

Open to girls aged 13–18 across the world. Applications take about 10 minutes — no essays, no grades required.

What to expect

You'll hear back within 2 weeks. We accept on a rolling basis — earlier applications get priority placement.

Need help? Email thivya.jeyapalan@capitalher.ca

Complete application on Google Forms

Applications are handled on Google Forms. You'll be asked for your name, email, city/country, age, program format (virtual or in-person Toronto/GTA), and why you want to join.
