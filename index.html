What we do

A program built around three pillars.

Financial literacy

Budgeting, investing, credit, and taxes — taught in language built for teen girls.

Entrepreneurship

From idea to MVP. Build a real business with mentors who've done it before.

Community

A national sisterhood of ambitious girls, mentors, and alumni rooting for you.

Our flagship program

The CapitalHER Accelerator.

A 12-week cohort program for girls aged 13–18. Weekly workshops on money and business, paired with a mentor and a real Demo Day pitch — online or in-person.

  • 12 weeks · weekly workshops
  • Online cohorts + in-person hubs in 6 cities
  • 1:1 mentorship with a woman in business
  • Demo Day pitch for a $10k funding pool
