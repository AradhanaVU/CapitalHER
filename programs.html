The Accelerator

12 weeks. Real skills.
A real business.

The CapitalHER Accelerator is our flagship program. Girls 13–18 build financial confidence and launch a real venture, supported by mentors who've done it.

What you'll learn

  • Personal finance: budgeting, credit, taxes, investing
  • Business model design & customer discovery
  • Branding, marketing & sales fundamentals
  • Pitching, negotiation, and public speaking
  • 1:1 mentorship from women founders & finance leaders
  • Demo Day with a real funding pool
